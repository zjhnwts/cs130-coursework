Student's Application Info

The Acalab Cross Science Summit is not for profit. Fees collected will be used for inviting tutors and professors, conference operations, room and board for students and team members, lab expenses and other materials.
We hope that every student with academic aspirations will have the opportunity to participate in the Summit, and we will try not to let the issue of funding become a factor limiting students' participation in the Summit. For special needs, Acalab 2019 will provide a certain amount of need-based Financial Aid as far as possible.
Regular application fee: ¥9200 Early application fee: ¥8200 Retention fee: ¥340
The early application deadline is June 30, 2019. Students should pay the deposit as soon as they receive the admission email. Acalab will hold a seat for 20 days. The reservation fee will eventually be deducted from the event registration fee. ​

Acalab 2021 Interdisciplinary Summit will be held in Shanghai, China from July 28 to May 8. Participants will be taught by scholars and professors from top universities at home and abroad to learn in-depth topics in the fields of humanities and science, including economics and sociology, psychology and cognitive science, philosophy and anthropology, biology and environmental science, and music production. During the nine days and eight nights of the activity, participants will systematically understand the background knowledge of the subject, design and complete a research topic of interest, and develop the ability of critical academic thinking and independent research.
